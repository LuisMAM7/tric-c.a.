About Us

TRIC, C.A. is a legally constituted Venezuelan company, dedicated to the design, supply, installation, and maintenance of power Self-Generation systems. es una empresa venezolana legalmente constituida, dedicada al diseño, suministro, instalación y mantenimiento de sistemas de Autogeneración de energía.

Our approach combines engineering, operational experience, and a network of international suppliers to offer reliable and scalable solutions.

We understand that the country's energy reality demands autonomy, operational reliability, and immediate response capacity. That is why we develop self-generation solutions between 375 and 2500 kVA..

Our Mission

To provide Self-Generation solutions that guarantee operational continuity to companies and organizations in Venezuela, through reliable, efficient energy projects adapted to the highest management standards.

Our Vision

To be a leading company in auxiliary energy and self-generation solutions in Venezuela, recognized for its technical capacity, commitment to quality, and contribution to the industrial and commercial development of the country.
